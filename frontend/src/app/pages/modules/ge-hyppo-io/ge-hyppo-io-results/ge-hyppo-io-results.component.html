Descriptions

Metadata:

SiteName_mod : Name of the project

Data_source : Name of the input file

Country :Country of the project

Equipment : GT frame

Customer : Customer Name

Controller : Name of the Operating Company

SiteName :Name of the Site

Serial_Number : Serial Number of the asset

Contract : Contract Information


Financial

Battery_price[$] : Initial price of the battery

Scen1_Saved_gas[kg] : Saved gas due to better efficiency in scenario 1

Scen2_more_pr_prft[$] : Profits gained selling more capacity reserve with scenario 2

Scen1_New_setpoint_prft[$] : Profits gained selling more electricity with scenario 2

SFFR_prft[$] : Profits gained selling the super fast frequency response service


Battery

Input

Apparent Power[MVA] : Apperent power needed for the battery, can be different from power when black start is required

Power_to_pr[MW]: Share of the power battery used to increase the capacity reserve

Power_to_load[MW] : Share of the power battery used to increase the load during primary response

Energy_max[MWh] : Size of the battery

Power[MW] : The power the battery is able to deliver

Power_super_fast_resp[MW] : The maximum power to deliver for the super fast frequency response


output

Mean_added_hr[MW/(kg/s)] : The mean added power over fuel ratio thanks to higher GT load

Mean_added_power[MW] : The mean added power thanks to the battery


stats

MAX_batt_Power[MW] : The max amount of battery power reached during primary response

Battery_losses[MWh] : The energy losses due to battery efficiency

GT_resp_time[%] : The percentage of the time the GT is used to provide primary response

MAX_SOC[%] : The max State Of Charge reached during primary response by the battery

min_SOC[%] : The min State Of Charge reached during primary response by the battery

Bat_resp_time[%] : The percentage of the time the Battery is used to provide primary response

SFFR_total_discharge[MWh] : The value of constant power to be purchased from the grid to end the period with the same battery energy level

SFFR_deadband_recharge_needed[MW] : The value of power to be purchased from the grid during the deadband period to end the period with the same battery energy level


Calc_param

baseload[MW] : Estimated baseload - calculated as the max of the 5 minutes rolling average of the power, when the power plant is not responding

primary_reserve[MW] : Estimated power plant capacity response - calculated as the baseload minus the max of the 5 minutes rolling average of the power, when the power plant is responding


Time_stats

Overall: The sum of all the time intervals analyzed

Start : The starting time of this time interval

Finish : The ending time of this time interval

Delta_time : Finish minus start time for this time interval
